# Okinawa.rbとは？
沖縄の Ruby/Rails コミュニュティです。沖縄に在住または滞在している Ruby/Rails な方々の情報交換を目的として、毎週水曜日の夜にギークハウス沖縄で Weekly Meetup を開いています。また、不定期にハッカソンや勉強会も開催しています。

沖縄在住の方でも、たまたま沖縄にフラっと寄っている方でも、どなたでも歓迎します。
Weekly Meetup に参加して頂けると大変嬉しいですが、Facebook Group に登録するだけな方も歓迎です！

# What's Okinawa.rb? 

Okinawa.rb is a local community for Ruby/Rails users in Okinawa, Japan. From beginners to veterans, all those who are interested in Ruby/Rails are welcome!

We have a [weekly meetup](http://www.okinawarb.org/meetups/) at [GeekHouse Okinawa](http://text.geeoki.com/info) __Every Wednesday Night (from 7pm)__ :)

## What's Weekly Meetup?

This is a weekly meetup hosted by Okinawa.rb and GeekHouse Okinawa, where people who like Ruby/Rails get together and casually share their information with snacks and drinks!

The meetup starts from about 7pm. Beginners are very welcome! If there is a new person, we'll introduce themselves each other and share what they'd like to do for the future.

Also, you can freely use wireless, power source, and a projector if you have something to share.

NOTE: You can't park at GeekHouse Okinawa. If you come here by car, you need to park at somewhere around it, such as Ryukyu Bank and Man-ko Park.
